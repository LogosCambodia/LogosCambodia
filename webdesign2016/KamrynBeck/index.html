Name: Kamryn T. Beck/ Age: 15/ Gender: Female/ Birth Date: April 7, 2000/ Nationality: American









Kamryn was born in California and moved to Cambodia two years later. She grew up going to Logos International School and though so young in her high school life, she was convicted of felonies beyond her years.





On November 20, 2015, she was caught impersonating a teacher. On November 27, she bore false witness concerning the whereabouts of her homework. On December 15, Kamryn was late in attending her classes twice in one day. And on January 6, 2016, she was caught, punished, and found indignant when she drew on school property.
